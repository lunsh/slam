About SLAM

The Somerville Local Art Mall is a pop-up market featuring the work of 23 local artists. Our goal is to create a space in vibrant Union Square to connect our fantastic population of creators with our fabulous population of creative consumers. The market will run for one month from mid-November to Mid-December, just in time for the holidays!
