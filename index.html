About SLAM

The Somerville Local Arts Mall is a pop-up market featuring the work of 23 local artists. Our goal is to create a space in vibrant Union Square to connect our fantastic population of creators with our fabulous population of creative consumers. The market will run for one month from mid-November to Mid-December, just in time for the holidays!

  • SLAM will be open 7 days a week November 20th through December 17th
  • Each artist will be renting their own room ranging in size and price to set up shop
  • All purchases will be made through a centralized point-of-sale using Square. We will share staffing responsibilities, so each artist staffs 4 days for a month of 7-days-a-week shopping exposure!
  • Deadline for applying is November 4th!
