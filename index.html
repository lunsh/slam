Welcome to the Somerville Local Arts Mall

a pop-up arts market that will take place from mid-November to mid-December in Union Square Somerville, MA.

About SLAM

The Somerville Local Art Mall is a pop-up market featuring the work of 23 local artists. Our goal is to create a space in vibrant Union Square to connect our fantastic population of creators with our fabulous population of creative consumers. The market will run for one month from mid-November to Mid-December, just in time for the holidays!

Check back soon for specific dates and the artist application! In the meantime, here are some key bits of relevant info:

  • SLAM will be open 7 days a week mid-November through mid-December
  • Each artist will be renting their own room ranging in size and price to set up shop
  • All purchases will be made through a centralized point-of-sale using Square. We will share staffing responsibilities, so each artist staffs 4 days for a month of 7-days-a-week shopping exposure!
  • It will be awesome!
